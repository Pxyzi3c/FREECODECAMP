HIDILYN DIAZ

The first Filipino Gold Medalist


About Hidilyn Diaz

Hidilyn Diaz is a Filipino Olympic weightlifter from Zamboanga City. Diaz won a bronze medal at the 2007 Southeast Asian Games in Thailand and finished 10th in the 53kg event at the 2006 Asian Games in Doha.

Birthdate : February 20, 1991

Birth place: Zamboanga City, Philippines

Weight : 58 kg

Height : 1.58 m

Rank : Military Ranks of the Philippines

Education : De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde Manila, Universidad de Zamboanga - City Campus

Parents : Eduardo Diaz Sr., Emelita Diaz
